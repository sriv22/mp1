Who we are

Students from the University of Illinois are working in close relation with Malaviya National Institute of Technology to solve India's most pressing issues: fluorosis and sanitation.
What we do
We'll be working in relation to the other students and devloping effective ways to address these issues. This includes creating proper indicators and/or building feasible toilets and water filtration systems.
